Introducing yourself and others; Asking for/giving basic personal information; Greetings

Introducing Yourself

Hello, I'm the new chief officer.

My name is Officer Lee, and I'll be joining your watch.

I'm pleased to meet you all.

Introducing Others

This is our chief engineer.

Let me introduce the navigation officer.

I'd like you to meet the new crew member.

Asking for Personal Information

What's your name and rank?

Where are you from?

How long have you been at sea?

Greetings

Good morning! How are you doing today?

Hi there! Everything okay?

Hello! How's your watch going?

Talking about daily work and leisure activities; Telling the time

Daily Work Activities

I start my watch at eight hundred hours.

We need to check the cargo securing.

I'm responsible for the bridge watch.

My duties include checking the navigation equipment.

Leisure Activities

During my free time, I usually read or exercise.

I like to watch movies in the evening.

We sometimes play cards in the crew mess.

Telling the Time

It's eight thirty in the morning.

The meeting starts at fourteen hundred hours.

We'll arrive around midnight.

My watch begins at zero four hundred.

Telephoning; Giving/receiving simple instructions; Solving minor problems

Telephoning

Could I speak to the chief officer, please?

This is the bridge calling the engine room.

Can you put me through to the captain?

I'll call you back in five minutes.

Giving Instructions

Please check the oil pressure gauge.

Make sure all the hatches are secured.

Monitor the weather forecast closely.

Solving Minor Problems

Understood. I'll do that right away.

I'll take care of it immediately.

Let me fix that for you.

I can handle this problem.

Making arrangements; Booking transport or berths; Discussing travel details

Making Arrangements

Can we schedule a meeting for tomorrow afternoon?

Let's arrange the cargo inspection for next Monday.

I'll organize the crew briefing.

Booking Transport or Berths

I would like to book a berth for three crew members.

We need to reserve transport to the airport.

Can you arrange accommodation for the pilots?

Discussing Travel Details

Our estimated arrival time is sixteen hundred hours.

The vessel will depart at dawn.

We're scheduled to reach port on Tuesday.

The journey takes approximately three days.

Ordering food; Making decisions in a group; Talking about likes/dislikes

Ordering Food

I'll have the grilled chicken, please.

Could I get a vegetarian option?

I'd like some coffee with breakfast.

What's the special today?

Making Group Decisions

What do you think about this plan?

Let's vote on the best option.

Does everyone agree with this approach?

We should consider all the alternatives.

Talking about Likes

I really enjoy working on deck.

I love the night watch because it's peaceful.

I prefer working in calm seas.

Talking about Dislikes

I'm not fond of rough weather.

I don't really like working in the engine room heat.

Heavy seas make the work more difficult.

Describing services; Making simple complaints; Requesting assistance

Describing Services

We provide twenty-four hour medical assistance on board.

Laundry service is available every Tuesday.

The mess is open from six to twenty hundred hours.

Making Simple Complaints

Excuse me, the air conditioning isn't working properly.

I'm afraid there's a problem with my cabin light.

The water pressure seems quite low.

Requesting Assistance

Could you help me with this, please?

I need some assistance with the equipment.

Would you mind giving me a hand?

Can someone help me carry these supplies?
